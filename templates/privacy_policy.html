Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how The Dayalbagh Consumers' Co-operative Society Limited ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information you provide when using the Fair Price Shop mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

When you register or use the App, we collect the following information:

  • Name — to identify you on orders and deliveries.
  • Phone Number — used as your unique login identifier.
  • Address — used to process and deliver your orders.
  • Gender (optional) — for account personalisation.
  • Order History — items ordered and their status.
  • Device Token — to send push notifications about your orders.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process and fulfil your orders.
  • To send order status notifications via push.
  • To allow admins to confirm orders and pricing.
  • To improve the App and our services.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on cloud servers (Supabase / PostgreSQL). Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We use token-based authentication to protect API access. We do not sell or rent your personal data to any third party.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third parties except:

  • As required by applicable law or government authority.
  • With our internal admin team solely to process your orders.

5. Push Notifications

The App uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send order-related notifications. You may disable notifications at any time from your device settings. Firebase's privacy policy applies to notification delivery.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
